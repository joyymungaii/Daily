Cute Bear

Bear cubs are called "cubs" for both male and female bears.
Bears can walk on their hind legs to get a better view or to
reach something high. Polar bears have black skin under their
white fur, which helps absorb heat from the sun.

Polar Bears

Polar bears have thick, fluffy coats to stay warm in the
Arctic. They love to eat ice and are excellent swimmers.
Polar bear cubs are very small when they are born.
They have dark brown eyes to help them see in the snow.
Polar bears are fascinating creatures adapted to life in the
harsh Arctic environment.

Spirit bear

Spirit bears, also known as Kermode bears, are a rare white variant of the
American black bear found in British Columbia, Canada. Their striking
white fur is caused by a recessive genetic mutation. Despite their unique
appearance, they are genetically identical to black bears. Spirit bears are
typically smaller than their black counterparts and have a slightly different
diet, often preferring salmon. These elusive creatures are a symbol of the
pristine wilderness of British Columbia and are closely protected by
conservation efforts.
